Blog

True stories of people who inspire in protecting and greening the Earth

Pandawara Group: Cleaning Up Indonesia’s Rivers and Beaches

Five young people from West Java who went viral for their river and beach clean-up efforts. They successfully inspired millions to care about the environment.

Jadav Payeng: Forest Man of India

An inspiring man who planted trees every day for decades, creating a vast forest in India, offering a lesson in perseverance and love for the environment.

Wangari Maathai: Green Belt Movement Kenya

The Nobel Peace Prize laureate who mobilized the people of Kenya to plant more than 50 million trees, transforming the face of the country while also empowering women.
